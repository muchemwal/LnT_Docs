# Privacy Policy

**L&T Transport Solutions Ltd**
**L&T Crew — Patient Transport App**

*Last updated: 20 June 2026*

## 1. Introduction

L&T Transport Solutions Ltd ("L&T", "we", "us", "our") operates the L&T Crew mobile application and web platform (the "Service") for managing patient transport operations across the United Kingdom.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect personal data when you use our Service. We are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR), the Data Protection Act 2018, and NHS data handling standards.

**Data Controller:** L&T Transport Solutions Ltd
**Contact:** privacy@landtts.co.uk

## 2. Data We Collect

### 2.1 Account Information
- Full name, email address, job role
- Login credentials (stored securely in Keycloak identity provider)
- Employment details (start date, contracted hours, certifications)

### 2.2 Location Data
- GPS coordinates (latitude, longitude, accuracy, heading, speed)
- Location timestamps
- Check-in/check-out positions

**When collected:** Only during active shift check-in and active patient transfers. Background location tracking occurs exclusively when a job status is "en route" or "in transit" and stops immediately upon job completion.

### 2.3 Operational Data
- Patient transfer form data (pickup/destination, patient reference, clinical notes)
- Job status history and timestamps
- Shift attendance records (check-in/out times, break durations)
- Vehicle inspection reports
- Incident reports

### 2.4 Communication Data
- In-app messages between crew and dispatch
- Voice messages (if push-to-talk feature is used)

### 2.5 Device Information
- Device type and operating system (for technical support)
- App version

### 2.6 Biometric Data
- Fingerprint or Face ID authentication tokens are stored locally on your device only (via iOS Keychain / Android Keystore). We never transmit biometric data to our servers.

## 6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures:

- All data encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3)
- Patient-identifiable fields encrypted at rest (AES-256)
- Authentication via Keycloak with MFA support
- Role-based access control (RBAC) — staff only see data relevant to their role
- Audit logging of all data access
- Infrastructure hosted in EU data centres
- Regular security assessments

## 8. Location Data — Specific Disclosures

**What:** GPS coordinates, speed, heading, and accuracy are collected every 5 seconds during active patient transfers.

**When:**
- **Foreground:** During shift check-in/check-out and active app use
- **Background:** ONLY when you have an active job with status "en route" or "in transit". A persistent notification is shown on Android when background tracking is active.

**Who sees it:**
- Dispatch staff (real-time, for job coordination)
- Patients with a shared tracking link (first name only, no surname or personal details)
- Your employer (historical records for compliance)

**How to stop:**
- Complete or cancel the active job
- Check out of your shift
- Revoke location permission in device Settings
